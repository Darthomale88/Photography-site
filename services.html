Booking & Payment

Flexible & straightforward

Booking is simple. A deposit holds your date, and I work with you on the rest.

Securing Your Date

A non-refundable retainer of 25–30% of your package total is required to officially reserve your session or wedding date. Dates are not held without a signed contract and deposit.

Popular dates fill 6–12 months in advance — book early to avoid missing out.

Payment Plans

The remaining balance can be split into installments leading up to your session date. Flexible schedules are available — just ask and we'll work something out that fits your budget.

Full balance is due no later than 7 days before your session date.

Accepted Methods

Payment is accepted via Venmo, Zelle, cash, or credit card. A small processing fee applies to credit card payments. All payments are confirmed in writing.
